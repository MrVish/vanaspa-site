Our Name

Vāna means forest

In Sanskrit and in Malayalam, vāna is the forest — and a Kerala forest at last light is exactly what we built this place to feel like. Deep greens. Warm amber. Air that asks nothing of you.

We are not a hotel spa, and we are not a clinic. Think of us the way you think of a favourite restaurant: a small place that does one beautiful thing properly, every single time. Here, that one thing is an hour that belongs entirely to you.

The VĀNA Way

We ask before you arrive

Three questions on WhatsApp the day before — how firm, where to focus, what to avoid. Two minutes of your time, so the hour is entirely yours.
