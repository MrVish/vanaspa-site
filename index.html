Our Name

Vāna means forest

In Sanskrit and in Malayalam, vāna is the forest — and a Kerala forest at last light is exactly what we built this place to feel like. Deep greens. Warm amber. Air that asks nothing of you.

We are not a hotel spa, and we are not a clinic. Think of us the way you think of a favourite restaurant: a small place that does one beautiful thing properly, every single time. Here, that one thing is an hour that belongs entirely to you.

The VĀNA Way

Your session begins
before you walk in

Three quiet courtesies that change everything — and that no other spa in Kakkanad offers. All three, every appointment.

01

We ask before you arrive

Three questions on WhatsApp the day before — how firm, where to focus, what to avoid. Two minutes of your time, so the hour is entirely yours.

02

Your therapist already knows

You walk in, and the session simply begins. No clipboard at reception, no questions at the table — your preferences arrived before you did.

03

We follow up the same evening

A short personal note after you leave: what was worked, what to stretch, what to carry into the week. Care that doesn't end at the door.

Quiet, precise, personal — the way it should have always worked.

Who It's For

people in mind

Not everyone, and that's the point. VĀNA was shaped around the people Kakkanad keeps underserving.

Solo, and safe

Women who want a serious massage without a second thought. Certified therapists, documented protocols, CCTV in common areas — never the rooms — and a choice of therapist where we can.

Desk-bound & aching

The Infopark shoulders, the laptop neck, the lower back that never quite lets go. Deep, deliberate work that actually reaches the knot you've been carrying for months.

The two of you

A dedicated couples suite for side-by-side hours — an anniversary, or just a Tuesday that earned it. The 120-minute Signature is the best room in the house.
